Helpful Methods and Fishing Tactics



Below you will find helpful information on how to sucessfully catch the fish displayed on my species webpage:



    Before you attempt to fish you will need the basic equipment such as:
  • Fishing rod and reel
  • Tackle box
  • Some sort of bait
  • Fishing pliers
  • Fishing Net
  • Propre saftey equipment
  • Boat if planning to fish from land




Northern Pike:

Catching methods: Attractions: Preferred Lures:
Trolling or Casting Shinny or Flashy lures Spoons, Crankbait, Meps


Largemouth Bass:

Catching methods: Attractions: Preferred Lures:
Trolling or Casting Bright or natural fish coloured lures Plastics/gummies (Crayfish, minnows/small fish) or Topwater (bugs and frogs)


Yellow Perch:

Catching methods: Attractions: Preferred Lures:
Trolling or Casting Small bright lures Small gummies (minnows) or Samll jigs


Lake Trout:

Catching methods: Attractions: Preferred Lures:
Deep trolling using sonar and downrigger Flashy or bright lures Spoons or longer crankbaits


Walleye/Pickerel:

Catching methods: Attractions: Preferred Lures:
Trolling or Casting Small bright lures Jigs, earth worms or crankbaits
